FAQ

How do internships work ?

During your fifth semester, a specific course will be given to help you get an internship. It is a 4-months paid internship. We have solid partners dating back to our foundation in 2000 in different multimedia companies of the region or in the federal sector. Most of the time, the interships transform into jobs. It is the start of a wonderfull multimedia carreer.

The multimedia program, is it a Technic DCS ?

Yes!
That means it is a 3 year course that lets you aqcuire the skills necessary for the job market. The advantage with us is 2½ years in school and your last semester in a paid internship! The ideal path to start a stimulating and financially successfull carreer!

Do I need to be good at programming or with ADOBE® software?

NO ! We start the course from scratch for everyone! But… if you would like to create interactive productions like websites and if you dream of learninf all sorts of graphic desing, programming, 3D modeling and animation, audio processing, video editing techniques and more… Well, you should really signup!

Is there going to be a lot of programming?

Yes.
Since the course largely uses web technologies, HTML, JavaScript and PHP/MySQL are necessary to support teh rendering, the animation and the interactivity of projects. The programming is always in service of the creation and allows for the realisation of beautifull and great things, weither it be a website, a microgame for mobile or an interactive installation.

Will there be lots of 3D?

Not very much. The course only includes 2 classes in 3D. However, the students willing to go further will always be supported by their teachers. Furthermore, since the domain of 3D and AAA games require specialised university studies, our course includes the creation of a personnal professional portfolio, which represents a great advantage for application on many of the concerned programs.

Is a creative or technical program?

It's both! Multimedia is a creative domaine in that it is necessary to create presentations, websites, applications or microgames from scratch. One therefore has to create media content like images, illustrations, animations. However, the program also presents a technical aspect as to be able to programm many aspects of one's creation with programming languages, which creates an opportunity for interactivity.

Are web technologies very present?

Yes! Since there does not exists a university course in web desing, we prepare our students to find a job in this expanding domain. However, our multidisciplinary course touches on many aspects and enables the application in many university programms in 3D, game design, graphic design and interactive media.
